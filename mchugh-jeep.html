Google Analytics & Search Console

📍 Currently Working On: Granting access to your Google Analytics and Search Console. This allows us to track organic traffic, keyword performance, and technical SEO health. Most clients complete this step in 5-10 minutes.
We need access to your analytics platforms to monitor SEO performance and make data-driven optimizations.
Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
  1. Go to analytics.google.com
  2. Click Admin (gear icon) → Property Access Management
  3. Click the blue + button → Add users
  4. Enter seo@carsignment.com, select Administrator, and click Add
Note: Your site uses multiple GA4 properties. Please grant access to your primary dealership property (likely labeled with your store name).
Google Search Console (GSC)
  1. Go to search.google.com/search-console
  2. Select your property (your website URL)
  3. Click Settings (left sidebar) → Users and permissions
  4. Click Add user, enter seo@carsignment.com, select Owner, and click Add
If you don't have Search Console set up yet: No problem! Let us know and we'll guide you through the setup process or handle it for you.

Google Business Profile & Tag Manager

📍 Currently Working On: Setting up access to your Google Business Profile (for local SEO) and optionally Tag Manager (for advanced tracking). These tools help us optimize your local search presence.
Local SEO requires management of your Google Business Profile. Tag Manager access is optional but helpful for advanced tracking.
Google Business Profile (GMB)
  1. Go to your Google Business Profile dashboard
  2. Click the three dots (Menu) → Business Profile settings → People and access
  3. Click Add, enter seo@carsignment.com
  4. Select Manager role and click Invite
Google Tag Manager (GTM)
  1. Go to tagmanager.google.com
  2. Click Admin → User Management
  3. Click the blue + button → Add users
  4. Enter seo@carsignment.com, select Administrator (ensure publish rights are checked), and click Invite
Optional: GTM access helps us implement advanced tracking and optimize conversion events. If you're unsure or prefer not to grant this now, we can work without it.
